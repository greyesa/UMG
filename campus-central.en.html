Services Center


Parking lot

Parking lot (Central Campus)

Description:
There are 9 parking areas for motorcycles and vehicles. Available for academic and administrative personnel.

Parking application procedure:

  • Request form
  • Bank agency payment.
  • Payment voucher presentation.
  • Granting of a parking tag to access the parking lot.
  • The form is uploaded to the database.
  • Forms filing.

Renew: Every semester.

There may be changes without prior notice.


Bank

Bank (Central Campus)

Bank agency:
Banco Industrial offers service to students and teachers. Opening hours: Monday to Friday: 08:30 to 20:00 hours and Saturdays 08:00 to 14:00 hours.

Location: At the side of building "A".

ATM:
To withdraw cash for students, faculty and administrative staff, Banco Industrial and 5B.

Location: ATM BI, next to the Banco Industrial bank agency. ATM 5B, next to the central cafeteria.

There may be changes without prior notice.
