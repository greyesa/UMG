Parking lot

Parking lot (Quetzaltenango Campus)

Description: For students, teachers and administrative staff. It has a parking lot for 500 vehicles, 15 motorcycles and 6 bicycles.

Address Parking lot 1: 1a. calle y 9a. Av. Los Alisos y Sauces, zone 1 La Esperanza, Quetzaltenango.

Address Parking lot 2: 2a. calle 0-79 zone 2, Quetzaltenango.

Parking application procedure:

  • Request form.
  • Bank agency payment.
  • Payment voucher presentation.
  • Granting of a parking tag to access the parking lot.

Renewal: Every semester .

There may be changes without prior notice.
